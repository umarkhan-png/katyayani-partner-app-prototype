Frequently Asked
ASK EXPERT
How much to mix per litre of water?
2 ml per litre of water. For 1 acre, 100–150 ml product mixed in 200 L water is standard.
Is it safe to spray during flowering?
Avoid spraying during peak flowering — bees get affected. Spray early morning or late evening.
Can I tank-mix with fungicide?
Yes — compatible with Katyayani Azodharma and most contact fungicides. Do a small jar test first.
