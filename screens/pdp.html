About this Product
Composition · Benefits · Mode

Katyayani Imida is a powerful systemic insecticide containing Imidacloprid 30.5% SC. Fast, long-lasting control against sucking pests — aphids, whitefly, jassids and thrips — across rice, cotton, vegetables, and other crops.

Apply as a foliar spray for quick absorption and residual protection. Safe for beneficial insects when used as directed. Suitable for Kharif and Rabi seasons.

Frequently Asked
ASK EXPERT
How much to mix per litre of water?
2 ml per litre of water. For 1 acre, 100–150 ml product mixed in 200 L water is standard.
Is it safe to spray during flowering?
Avoid spraying during peak flowering — bees get affected. Spray early morning or late evening.
Can I tank-mix with fungicide?
Yes — compatible with Katyayani Azodharma and most contact fungicides. Do a small jar test first.
Bill mein GST laga hoga kya?
