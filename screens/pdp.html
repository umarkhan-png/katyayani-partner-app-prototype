Katyayani Imida is a powerful systemic insecticide containing Imidacloprid 30.5% SC. It provides fast, long-lasting control against sucking pests like aphids, whitefly, jassids and thrips on rice, cotton, vegetables, and other crops.

Apply as a foliar spray for quick absorption and residual protection. Safe for beneficial insects when used as directed. Suitable for both Kharif and Rabi seasons.
