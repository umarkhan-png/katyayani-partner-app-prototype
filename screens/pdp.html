Katyayani Imida is a powerful systemic insecticide containing Imidacloprid 30.5% SC. Fast, long-lasting control against sucking pests — aphids, whitefly, jassids and thrips — across rice, cotton, vegetables and other crops.

Apply as a foliar spray for quick absorption and residual protection. Safe for beneficial insects when used as directed. Suitable for Kharif and Rabi seasons.
